MASSA Club App

Your one-stop app for managing a club

A Flutter mobile application that provides everything you wanted for managing your clubs and activities.

Still managing clubs manually?

Managing people and events are hard. University clubs are still managing events and its committee members and their tasks using different communication platforms and tools. Wonder why no one joins your events?

Do a lot better with our app.

This app provides everything you wanted for managing a university club, including hosting and managing events with ease, as well as keep track of your committee members to ensure responsibility.

Features

Event Management

Create and manage your events easily.

Exco Manage

Manage and monitor your executive committee members.

Event Calendar

See upcoming events in your club and never miss out again.

Smart Notifications

Get reminders on newly created events and increase engagement.
